About KrushakFarm

Our Mission: Bridging the gap between hardworking farmers and mindful consumers.

We started KrushakFarm with a simple vision: to create a fair, transparent marketplace where farmers get the right price for their harvest, and consumers get access to farm-fresh, sustainable produce. By eliminating the middlemen, we ensure quality and fairness every step of the way.

  • 🌿 100% Fresh & Local
  • 🤝 Fair Trade for Farmers
  • 🌱 Sustainable Agriculture
