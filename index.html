Frequently Asked Questions

How do I order from KrushakFarm?

+

Simply browse our categories or search for specific products, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You will need to create a free account to complete your purchase.

Are the products organically grown?

+

We work closely with farmers who prioritize organic and sustainable farming practices. Check the product details or browse our "Organic Veggies" category for certified organic items.

How quickly will I receive my order?

+

Because we source directly from local farms, most orders are harvested and delivered within 24 to 48 hours to guarantee maximum freshness.
